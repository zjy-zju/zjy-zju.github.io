About Me

I am an MD student at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Zhaopeng Cui. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. I have a broad interest in 3D vision technologies and its AR/VR applications, including neural rendering and 3D scene editing.
